Peanut Butter Blossoms

Ingredients

  • 1 c shortening
  • 1 c peanut butter
  • 1 t salt
  • 2 t vanilla
  • 1 c white sugar
  • 1 c packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 c milk
  • 3 & 1/2 c flour
  • 2 t baking soda
  • 2 (9 oz.) bags milk chocolate candy kisses, unwrapped

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375.

Grease cookie sheets.

Cream shortening, peanut butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until smooth.

Beat in eggs, one at a time.

Stir in milk and vanilla.

In a seperate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.

Stir flour mixture into the peanut butter mixture until well blended.

Shape tablespoons of dough into balls.

Place cookies on cookie sheet 2 inches apart.

Bake on center rack for 10 to 12 minutes.

Remove cookies from the over and immediately press a chocolate kiss into the cookie.

Allow cookies and chocolate kisses to cool. Then, serve.
